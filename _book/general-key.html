Winter Botany in the British Isles

General Key

  1. Leaves evergreen, needle like, scale like or both, usually hard, mostly resinous or odorous. Reproducing through cones -> Key 1 (Conifers)

  2. Leaves evergreen, deciduous or absent, not needle or scale like, resinous or not. Reproducing (almost exclusively) through flowers and fruits -> 3 (Flowering plants)

    1. Leaves evergreen and parallel veined -> Key 2
    2. If leaves evergreen, then not parallel veined -> 5
  3. Leaves or leaf scars >2 per node (whorled, pseudowhorled or in bundles) -> Key 3

  4. Leaves or leaf scars <3 per node -> 7

    1. Leaves or leaf scars 2 per node (opposite or subopposite) -> Key 4
    2. Leaves or leaf scars 1 per node (alternate) -> Key 5
